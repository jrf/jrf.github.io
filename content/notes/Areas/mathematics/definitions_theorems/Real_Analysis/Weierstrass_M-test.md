# Weierstrass_M-Test

## Corollary

For each $n \in \mathbb{N}$, let $f_{n}$ be a [function](/notes/areas/mathematics/definitions_theorems/set_theory/function/) defined on a set $A \subseteq \mathbb{R}$, and let $M_{n} > 0$ be a real number satisfying



$$
\left| f_{n}\left. (x) \right. \right| \leq M_{n}
$$



for all $x \in A$. If $\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}M_{n}$ converges, then $\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}f_{n}$ converges uniformly on $A$.
