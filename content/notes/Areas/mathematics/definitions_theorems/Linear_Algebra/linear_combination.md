# Linear_combination

## Definition

A *linear combination* of a [list](/notes/areas/mathematics/definitions_theorems/linear_algebra/list/) $v_{1},\ldots,v_{m}$ of vectors in $V$ is a vector of the form



$$
a_{1}v_{1} + \cdots + a_{m}v_{m}
$$



where $a_{1},\ldots,a_{m} \in \mathbb{F}$.
