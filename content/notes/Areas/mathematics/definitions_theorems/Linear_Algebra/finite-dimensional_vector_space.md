# Finite-dimensional_vector_space

## Definition

A [vector_space](/notes/areas/mathematics/definitions_theorems/linear_algebra/vector_space/) is called *finite-dimensional* if some [list](/notes/areas/mathematics/definitions_theorems/linear_algebra/list/) of vectors in it [span](/notes/areas/mathematics/definitions_theorems/linear_algebra/span/)s the space.
