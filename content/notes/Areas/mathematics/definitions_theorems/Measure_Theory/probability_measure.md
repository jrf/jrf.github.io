# Probability_measure

## Definition

Let a [measure](/notes/areas/mathematics/definitions_theorems/measure_theory/measure/) $\mu$ on $X$ be a map. Then, a *probability measure* is a [measure](/notes/areas/mathematics/definitions_theorems/measure_theory/measure/) with $\mu\left. (\varnothing) \right. = 0$ and $\mu\left. (X) \right. = 1$.
