# Bijection

## Definition

A [function](/notes/areas/mathematics/definitions_theorems/set_theory/function/) that is both injective and surjective.

In other words, a [function](/notes/areas/mathematics/definitions_theorems/set_theory/function/) that is both one-to-one and onto.
